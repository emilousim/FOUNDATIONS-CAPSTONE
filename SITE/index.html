HELPING LOUISIANA'S FELINE FRIENDS PUT THEIR BEST PAW FORWARD SINCE 2021

We are Louisiana's #1 TNR program and no-kill, open intake shelter.

Feline Friends 4EVER is Louisiana's leading no-kill community for cats and kittens by providing care, shelter, and adoption services. As well as providing a sanctuary for unwanted cats, Fierce Feline Rescue's work with feral cats involves spaying and neutering complete colonies and giving any necessary veterinary treatment. Feral cats are returned to their site of capture if long-term food and shelter can be provided. If not, they remain at FFR for life.

Feline Friends Forever was founded in 2021 by Emily Simpson in order to fulfill the desperate need in Southeastern Louisiana for a TNR (trap, neuter, release) program. FF4Ever also has its own adoption program to help cats and kittens find their fur-ever homes.
